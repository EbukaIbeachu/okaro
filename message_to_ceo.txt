Dear CEO,

I am pleased to formally present the beta deployment of the new Property Management System, custom-engineered for Okaro & Associates. This platform is designed to modernize operations, enhance tenant relations, and provide comprehensive oversight of the property portfolio.

**System Overview & Strategic Value:**

This solution transitions core management workflows into a secure, digital environment:

1.  **Centralized Command Dashboard:** A unified interface for real-time monitoring of properties, units, and lease statuses.
2.  **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):**
    *   **Admins:** Complete system sovereignty, including user verification, data governance, and configuration.
    *   **Managers:** Operational access focused on tenant onboarding and maintenance, safeguarded against system-wide configuration changes.
    *   **Tenants:** A dedicated, secure portal for lease review, payment history tracking, and maintenance reporting.
3.  **Verified Onboarding Protocol:** Implementation of a strict Admin-approval workflow for new registrations, ensuring data security and authorized access only.
4.  **Financial Integrity:** Automated lease tracking and payment logging to minimize arrears and streamline financial reporting.
5.  **Maintenance Resolution Cycle:** Digital ticketing system to track repair requests from submission to resolution, improving property upkeep efficiency.
